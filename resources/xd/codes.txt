LOT8s Background Codes by Dalk
Few things to note:
* You have to cue these under logo in Load Run Playlist (domesticAds/TAG<code>)
* Some of these have MSO backgrounds. This means that for certain MSO codes, you will get backgrounds from a certain providor.
* 3092 and Spectrum contains the Spectrum houses.
* This took hours to compile. Do not share.
* Made by the favorite minnesotan
* MSO Codes are below:
Spectrum Charter: 01148
Xfinity Comcast: 01174
Cox: 01179
Verizon FiOs: 03467
Optimum: 01181
Suddenlink: 03363
